 - systolic blood pressure (mmHg)
 - diastolic blood pressure (mmHg)
 - heart rate (bpm)

 - systolic: 124
 - diastolic: 79
 - rate: 64

After you have logged the heart metrics, respond to the user with a message stating that you have successfully logged the metrics and also provide the values, so they can confirm you understood.
